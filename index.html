How to Play

Planning Mode

  • Place the top of the ship on your grid by clicking on a tile. You can change the direction the ship will point by pressing "r"
  • Once you are happy with where you placed the ship, confrim the placement by pushing the button.

Battle Mode

  • In battle mode you need to work out where the computer has hidden the ships and destroy them before your ships get sunk.
  • Select a tile on the computer's grid to choose where to shoot at then confrim your shot.
  • Your radar will tell you if it was a hit or a miss. If you sink a ship it will also be displayed what ship was sunk
  • The computer's radar will tell you the same information about the shots against you.
  • Destroyer: length 2
  • Submarine: length 3
  • Cruiser: length 3
  • Battleship: length 4
  • Carrier: length 5
